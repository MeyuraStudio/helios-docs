Project Helios

Purpose of the Application

Project Helios is a proprietary, internal automation tool developed and maintained by Meyura Studio. Its primary purpose is to streamline our internal video content generation workflow. It automates the scheduling and publishing of video assets to our authorized YouTube channels by securely reading operational data from Google Sheets and retrieving assets from Google Drive.

Note: This application is strictly for internal administrative use by Meyura Studio and is not available to the general public.
